My Beats

The Essential Collection

Welcome to My Beats, a personal digital jukebox showcasing the soundtracks of my creative life. Music is the rhythm behind every line of code and every design I build. This collection features my definitive Top 5 tracks from the industry's most influential titans: Chris Brown and Drake.

Chris Brown Drake

Chris Brown

Commonly referred to as the "King of R&B," Chris Brown has built a legacy on unparalleled performance and vocal versatility. Since his 2005 debut, he has redefined the genre by blending soulful R&B with pop and hip-hop, amassing a record-breaking number of hits and influencing a whole generation of artists.

Top 5 Picks

Residuals

11:11 | 2023 | R&B
Personal Vibe: The vocal layering in this track is incredible. It’s my go-to late-night song because it perfectly captures a moody, introspective R&B atmosphere.

Grass Ain't Greener

Heartbreak on a Full Moon | 2017
Personal Vibe: This is a classic "toxic R&B" anthem. The production is dark and hypnotic, making it a standout from the massive Heartbreak on a Full Moon album.

WGFT (Remix)

R&B / Collaboration
Personal Vibe: I love the smooth, collaborative energy here. It’s one of those tracks where the harmonies are so clean you can listen to it on loop while working.

Go Crazy

Slime & B | 2020 | Hip-Hop
Personal Vibe: Pure energy. This song stayed at the top of my charts because the beat is so infectious—it’s impossible not to move when this comes on.

Angel Numbers / Ten Toes

11:11 | 2023 | R&B
Personal Vibe: I appreciate the transition in this track. It feels like two different moods combined into one masterpiece, showing how much Chris has evolved.

Drake

The architect of the modern streaming era, Drake has redefined the sound of global music. By fusing vulnerable storytelling with aggressive rap anthems, he has created a sound that is uniquely his own, serving as a cultural bridge across continents and genres.

Top 5 Picks

Time Flies

Dark Lane Demo Tapes | 2020
Personal Vibe: This is peak "lo-fi" Drake. It’s the ultimate chill song for coding; the mellow production helps me focus without distracting from the task.

Passionfruit

More Life | 2017 | R&B
Personal Vibe: The tropical, dancehall-inspired rhythm makes this an all-time summer favorite. It’s timeless, lighthearted, and always feels fresh.

Unforgettable

Thank Me Later | 2018
Personal Vibe: This track brings back great memories. The melody is catchy, and it captures that early era of Drake’s global dominance perfectly.

Headlines

Take Care | 2011 | Hip-Hop
Personal Vibe: A total nostalgia trip. This was Drake at his most confident, and the beat still sounds iconic over a decade later.

Best Thing I Ever Had

Thank Me Later | 2018
Personal Vibe: I love the mix of storytelling and rap here. It’s a great example of Drake’s ability to talk about personal relationships in a way that’s relatable.
